## UPDATED App Review Explanation (max 1000 chars):
```
PharaohicSoul is a social commerce platform for handmade product sellers. It enables sellers to showcase their products on TikTok by creating and publishing product videos directly from the platform.

How it works:
1. Sellers connect their TikTok account via Login Kit (user.info.basic scope).
2. Sellers create product showcase videos using the platform's content tools.
3. Sellers preview the content and choose privacy settings before posting.
4. The Content Posting API (video.upload scope) uploads the video to TikTok as a draft for the seller to review and publish.
5. Sellers retain full control — they can edit, approve, or discard content before it goes live.

The platform currently serves handmade product sellers in the Egyptian artisan and home decor niche, with plans to expand to more seller categories. All content is user-generated and original. The platform does not auto-post without user consent.

Website: https://hany019.github.io/pharaohicsoul-site/
```
