Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 19, 2026

1. Introduction

PharaohicSoul ("we", "our", "us") is a social commerce platform that helps sellers publish product videos to TikTok. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website, dashboard, or services.

2. Information We Collect

When you use PharaohicSoul, we may collect: account information (name, email) when you register; TikTok profile information (username, avatar, open ID) when you connect via Login Kit; content you create through our platform (videos, captions, hashtags); usage analytics (pages visited, features used); and information you voluntarily provide through support requests.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to: provide and operate the PharaohicSoul platform; authenticate your TikTok account via Login Kit (user.info.basic scope); upload and publish content to TikTok on your behalf via Content Posting API (video.upload scope); display your content status and analytics in the dashboard; improve our platform and user experience; send service-related communications; and comply with legal obligations.

4. TikTok Data Handling

When you connect your TikTok account, we access only the data permitted by the scopes you authorize. We use user.info.basic to verify your identity and display your connected account. We use video.upload to upload content you create to TikTok as drafts for your review. We do not access your TikTok messages, followers list, or any data beyond the authorized scopes. We do not sell TikTok user data to any third party. You can disconnect your TikTok account at any time from the Dashboard settings or directly from TikTok's authorization management page.

5. Data Storage and Security

We store your data on secure servers with encryption in transit and at rest. TikTok access tokens are stored securely and are never exposed in client-side code. We retain your data only for as long as your account is active. Upon account deletion, we remove all associated data within 30 days.

6. Third-Party Services

PharaohicSoul integrates with TikTok (via TikTok for Developers APIs), Etsy, and Amazon. Each platform has its own privacy policy governing how they handle your data. We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

7. Cookies

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to maintain your session, remember your preferences, and collect analytics data. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

8. Your Rights (GDPR)

Under applicable privacy laws, including the GDPR for EU residents, you have the right to: access your personal data; request correction or deletion of your data; export your data in a portable format; object to data processing; withdraw consent at any time; and disconnect third-party integrations. To exercise these rights, contact us at pharaohicsoul@gmail.com or use the account settings in your Dashboard.

9. Children's Privacy

PharaohicSoul is not directed at children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a minor has created an account, please contact us immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes become effective upon posting. We will notify registered users of significant changes via email.

11. Contact Us

For questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at pharaohicsoul@gmail.com.
