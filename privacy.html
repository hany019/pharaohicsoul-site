Last updated: March 13, 2026

1. Introduction

PharaohicSoul ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit our website, interact with our social media content, or use our services.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information: basic contact information (name, email) when you contact us directly; order information processed through Etsy's platform; analytics data from our website (page views, visit duration) through standard web analytics; and information you voluntarily provide through social media interactions.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to: respond to your inquiries and provide customer support; process and fulfill orders through our Etsy shop; improve our website and social media content; create and publish promotional content on platforms including TikTok, Instagram, and Pinterest; and comply with legal obligations.

4. TikTok Integration

PharaohicSoul uses the TikTok Content Posting API to publish product videos and promotional content to our TikTok account. This integration allows us to share content directly from our content management system. We do not collect personal data from TikTok users through this integration. When you interact with our TikTok content, TikTok's own Privacy Policy applies.

5. Third-Party Services

Our services integrate with third-party platforms including Etsy, TikTok, Instagram, and Pinterest. Each platform has its own privacy policy governing how they handle your data. We encourage you to review these policies. We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information we collect. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

7. Cookies

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience and collect analytics data. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

8. Your Rights

Under applicable privacy laws (including the GDPR for EU residents), you have the right to: access your personal data; request correction or deletion of your data; object to data processing; and withdraw consent at any time. To exercise these rights, contact us at pharaohicsoul@gmail.com.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes become effective upon posting to this website. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

11. Contact Us

For questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at pharaohicsoul@gmail.com. PharaohicSoul is operated from Sevilla, Spain.
